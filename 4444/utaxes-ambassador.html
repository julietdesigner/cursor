04Frequently Asked Questions

What makes a successful referral?

A successful referral must:
- Sign up using your unique referral link
- Complete a paid tax filing (Federal or State)
- Be a U.S. resident or business
- Be a first-time uTaxes user

How and when do I get paid?

Payments are processed via direct deposit or PayPal within 7-14 business days after the following month of completed tax filing. Identity verification (KYC) is required for compliance with U.S. tax regulations.

What tax services are eligible?

All paid tax services including:
- Federal individual tax returns
- State tax returns
- Business tax filings (LLC, S-Corp, C-Corp)
- Self-employed and freelancer taxes
