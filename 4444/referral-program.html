FAQ

Who can join the program?
expand_more
Anyone can join our ambassador program! Whether you're an influencer, content creator, or just someone with a network of friends who could benefit from our service.
Referral Payout
expand_more
Payments are processed weekly. Once your referral completes a qualifying action, the commission will be added to your account and paid out in the next payment cycle.
What is a referral?
expand_more
A referral is someone who signs up using your unique referral link and completes the required actions to qualify for the program.
Does my referral receive any benefits?
expand_more
Yes! Your referrals receive a special welcome bonus when they sign up through your link, giving them extra value.
What is a "closed deal"?
expand_more
A closed deal occurs when your referral completes a purchase or transaction that meets our minimum qualifying amount of $50.
